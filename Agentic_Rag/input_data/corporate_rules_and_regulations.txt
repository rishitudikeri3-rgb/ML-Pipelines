                    AETHERFLOW TECHNOLOGIES HANDBOOK
                      Corporate Rules & Regulations
================================================================================

Welcome to AetherFlow Technologies. This document outlines the official corporate
rules, regulations, and operational policies governing all employees, contractors,
and representatives of the company.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. MISSION & VALUES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
At AetherFlow Technologies, our mission is to deliver seamless cloud optimization
and advanced AI integration solutions. We operate on three core values:
- Innovation: Continuously pushing the boundaries of what is possible in tech.
- Integrity: Holding ourselves to the highest ethical and professional standards.
- Inclusivity: Cultivating an environment where diverse perspectives are celebrated.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. WORKPLACE STRUCTURE & EXPECTATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 Hybrid Work Model
AetherFlow operates on a hybrid model to balance collaboration and flexibility.
- Mandatory Collaboration Days: Tuesdays and Thursdays (all employees must work
  from their designated AetherFlow regional office).
- Flexible Work Days: Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays (employees may choose
  to work remotely or from the office).

2.2 Core Hours
Regardless of location, all employees must be active and accessible during our
Core Collaboration Hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM local time. Standard team standups
and client alignment sessions must be scheduled during this window.

2.3 Time Tracking
All hourly employees and contractors must log their billable hours in the internal
work tracking portal by Friday 5:00 PM each week.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. CODE OF CONDUCT & PROFESSIONALISM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 Anti-Harassment & Non-Discrimination
AetherFlow is dedicated to providing a harassment-free workplace for everyone. We do
not tolerate harassment or discrimination of any kind, including but not limited to
race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or religion.

3.2 Professional Communication
All digital communication (Slack, Email, Teams) must remain professional and respectful.
Keep video cameras turned on during key external client-facing calls to maintain
strong engagement and representation.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. SECURITY & DATA PRIVACY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.1 AI & LLM Usage Policy
While we encourage the exploration of AI tools, employees must NEVER paste proprietary
source code, client data, intellectual property, or confidential financial metrics
into public LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini free tiers) unless using the company-
approved, enterprise-secured internal LLM portal.

4.2 Device Security
All company work must be conducted on encrypted corporate-issued laptops. Personal
devices (BYOD) are restricted from accessing internal code repositories.

4.3 VPN Usage
When working from remote locations, public cafes, or hotels, employees are required
to activate the AetherFlow secure VPN before accessing company networks or cloud consoles.

4.4 Clean Desk Policy
To prevent data exposure, employees must not leave sensitive printed materials, passwords,
or security keycards unattended on their desks.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY (IP)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
All source code, design mockups, AI models, prompts, data pipelines, and internal
documentation created by employees during their tenure at AetherFlow Technologies
remain the exclusive intellectual property of the company.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. DRESS CODE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Our office environment is "smart-casual." Neat jeans, casual shirts, and sneakers
are fully acceptable. However, for face-to-face client meetings, business-professional
attire is expected.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. BENEFITS, STIPENDS, & EXPENSES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7.1 Self-Care Stipend
Employees are eligible for a $100/month wellness stipend to be used toward gym
memberships, mental health apps, or physical well-being.

7.2 Professional Development
AetherFlow offers up to $1,500 annually per employee for approved technical courses,
conferences, books, or certification exams.

7.3 Expense Submissions
All business-related expenses must be submitted with valid receipts via the internal
expensing portal by the 25th of each calendar month. Late submissions will be
processed in the following month's payroll cycle.
